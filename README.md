# CFD Erosion Modeling

## Overview

This project summarizes CFD work related to particle-laden internal flows and erosion prediction. The focus is on building simulation workflows that connect geometry preparation, meshing, boundary-condition definition, particle tracking, validation, and engineering interpretation.

## Engineering Problem

Industrial piping and flow equipment can experience erosion when solid particles impact internal walls at high velocity or unfavorable angles. CFD can help identify high-risk regions, compare operating conditions, and guide design or maintenance decisions.

## Methods

- Prepared simulation-ready geometry from mechanical flow components
- Generated structured or high-quality computational meshes
- Defined turbulence, wall treatment, inlet/outlet, and particle-injection conditions
- Used particle tracking to study impact behavior and likely erosion zones
- Compared simulation output with experimental or literature trends where available
- Post-processed contours, line probes, particle tracks, and engineering plots
